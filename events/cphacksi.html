About

CP-Hacks is an all-girls, student-led, nonprofit virtual hackathon that aims to empower middle and high school girls to develop their programming skills. Like our other programs, Codepioneers seeks to provide an avenue for students to pursue a career in STEM. In this hackathon, teams will have 24 hours to design, program, and present a project that solves a real-world problem.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where and when is CP-Hacks?

CP-Hacks will be hosted virtually over Zoom on August 7-8, 2021!

What is our goal?

CP-Hacks is a student-led, nonprofit virtual hackathon that aims to empower middle and high school students to develop their programming skills. Like our other programs, Codepioneers seeks to provide an avenue for students to pursue a career in STEM.

Who can attend?

Students from grades 6-12 who identify as female or non-binary are welcome to attend!

How do I sign up?

Sign up by filling out our registration form.

How much will it cost?

Nothing! This hackathon is absolutely free!

Do I need programming experience?

Absolutely not! We will be offering a variety of workshops, and mentors to help you personally. We will also be hosting one-week boot camps the week before, which are meant specifically for beginner programmers to learn Python/Web Development!

Do I need to have a team?

You do not need to have a team! We will help you form teams at the start of the hackathon if you do not have one. Teams will be 1-4 people.

How do I become a mentor/judge?

We would love to have you as a mentor or judge! Please sign up here!

Is there a Code of Conduct?

Yes! All participants must abide by the Codepioneers Code of Conduct.
